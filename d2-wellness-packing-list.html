In-Flight Wellness · Packing List

The night-before
wellness checklist.

Everything your dog needs for a comfortable flight — in one list. Pack this in your carry-on, not checked luggage.

Medications
Cerenia (maropitant)Rx Anti-nausea. Give 2 hrs before departure.
GabapentinRx Flight-day anxiety dose. Not the same as daily arthritis dose — confirm with vet.
Melatonin (natural remedy — discuss with vet first)Some owners use 0.5–1mg for small dogs. Zac didn\'t need it.
Ginger (natural remedy — discuss with vet first)Some owners use ~250mg for nausea. Zac didn\'t need it.
Chews & Food
2× bully sticks One for ascent, one for descent. The descent one matters more.
Backup chew (Himalayan / Kong) In case the first is refused. Both introduced at home beforehand.
High-value small treats For giving medications and reward. Not for feeding.
Collapsible bowl + small water bottle Offer water during cruise. Hydration helps nausea.
Comfort & Calm
Thundershirt On 15–20 mins before boarding, not at the gate.
Worn t-shirt or familiar blanket Your scent in the carrier. Do this every time.
Bach Rescue Remedy 2–4 drops on gums at boarding. Non-sedating.
Favourite toy Familiar smell and texture. Something they already love.
Documents
Travel health certificate Issued within 10 days of departure.
Vaccination + titer records
Medication list with doses Written copy for customs if asked.
Vet contact number For remote advice in transit if needed.
At Destination
Ear cleaning solution — gentle post-flight clean. Check for redness or discharge in the hours after landing.
Probiotic supplement — travel disrupts gut flora. Start on arrival, continue a few days.
Flight day timing — at a glance
T–8 hrs Last full meal. Water freely after.
T–2 hrs Cerenia (if prescribed).
T–1 hr Gabapentin (if prescribed). Body wrap or Thundershirt.
Descent Second chew in. Ear Slides. Full protocol.
All medications require vet sign-off. Trial gabapentin at home at least one week before the flight — first doses can cause pronounced sedation. The full wellness protocol is in The Flight Plan.
